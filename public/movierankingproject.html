Movies That I Have Watched!

These Are The Best Movies Of All Time And I Suggest Everyone To Watch Them

#1 Rise of the Planet of the Apes

WHY TO WATCH?

CBFC: U/A 2011 ‧ Action/Sci-fi ‧ 1h 45m

"Rise of the Planet of the Apes" is worth watching for its compelling storyline, impressive visual effects, and strong performances, particularly by Andy Serkis as Caesar. The film offers a thought-provoking exploration of intelligence and rebellion, making it both entertaining and intellectually engaging.

#2 Dawn of the Planet of the Apes

WHY TO WATCH?

CBFC: U/A 2014 ‧ Action/Sci-fi ‧ 2h 10m

"Dawn of the Planet of the Apes" is worth watching for its intense action, tunning visual effects, and deep emotional storytelling. It explores themes of survival, trust, and conflict, offering a gripping continuation of Caesar's journey and the evolving dynamic between humans and apes.

#3 War for the Planet of the Apes

WHY TO WATCH

CBFC: U/A 2017 ‧ Action/Sci-fi ‧ 2h 20m

"War for the Planet of the Apes" is a must-watch for its powerful narrative, breathtaking visuals, and emotional depth. The film delivers a poignant conclusion to Caesar's saga, exploring themes of revenge, sacrifice, and humanity. Its compelling performances and epic battles make it a standout in the trilogy.
